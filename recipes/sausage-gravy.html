Sausage Gravy

Super Savory Sausage Gravy

A delicious sausage gravy recipe that goes great on biscuits, potatoes, or anything else that uses gravy.

You'll be the talk of the town with this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 3 tablespoons white flour
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Basil
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Fry the Italian sausage on medium heat in a large frying pan until brown.
  2. Drain greese from frying pan into a cup or bowl and set aside.
  3. Remove sausage from frying pan and set aside.
  4. Pour sausage grease back into the frying pan, and on medium-low heat, slowly stir flour into the sausage grease until grease is fully absorbed into the flour.
  5. Slowly add milk, stirring constantly.
  6. Still stirring, add bouillon.
  7. Add garlic powder, onion powder, and basil to taste.
  8. Little-by-little add water and continue stirring until gravy reaches your desired thickness.
  9. Remove from heat.
  10. Smother over your favorite food, and enjoy this guilty pleasure.
