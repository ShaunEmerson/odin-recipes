Saucy Beef Stroganoff

A delicious sausage gravy recipe that goes great on biscuits, potatoes, eggs, toast, or anything else.

You'll be the talk of the town with this amazing recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. sliced steak
  • 1 1/2 cubes butter
  • 3 tablespoons white flour
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Beef bouillon
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped bell pepper
  • 1 lb. sliced mushrooms
  • 1 package extra-wide egg noodles
  • Sour cream
  • 3 cups water

Steps

  1. Boil the package of noodles until tender.
  2. Drain noodles, stir in half-stick of butter.
  3. Sprinkle noodles with paprika and set aside.
  4. In a large deep frying pan, melt a half-stick butter on medium heat.
  5. Add the onions, bell pepper, and mushrooms, and sautee until onions are caramelized.
  6. Add the steak and cook until steak is brown.
  7. Reduce heat to medium low, add one cup water and continue cooking for ten minutes.
  8. in a separate frying pan melt the remaining half-stick of butter, and stir in the white flour to create a rue.
  9. Add the rue to the onions, bell pepper, mushrooms, and steak.
  10. Slowly stir in the remaining two cups of water into the rue.
  11. Stir in one teaspoon garlic powder.
  12. Add in beef bouillon until the mixture tastes slightly too salty.
  13. Continue cooking and stirring the sauce until it reduces down and becomes thick
  14. Add a few teaspoons of sour cream until the sauce tastes slightly sour and salty.
  15. Remove from heat.
  16. Pile noodles on plate and smother with sauce.
  17. Enjoy!
